VHF/UHF

9 July 2021 • 1 min read

For VHF/UHF/D-STAR I have the Icom ID-5100A Deluxe. It is a 2m/70cm/D-STAR radio which also can be used for public service monitoring.

For an antenna I use a Diamond X-50NA Dualband antenna. It is mounted on my chimney and is about 6ft tall with a 10ft mast.
